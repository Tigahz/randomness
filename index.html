How do I use the website?

To use the website, you simply have to hover over the navbar and click on one of the pages. There you will be redirected to a 'hub' page. These 'hub' pages can redirect you to a page about a ship, or a class of ship - really whatever you press.

REMEMBER: The navbar will always be at the top of the screen!

About Us

We are a site that contains naval history from WWI & WWII, through the Cold War, right to present day navies. The website has been created entirely with CSS3 and HTML5.
